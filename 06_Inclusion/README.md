# Inclusion

## Overview
Uses data to surface hidden inequities in hiring, promotion, pay, and retention. Moves inclusion from narrative to measurable outcomes with accountability.

## Business Impact
- Diverse teams outperform homogeneous ones by 35% (McKinsey, 2024)
- Diverse teams generate 19% higher revenue from innovation (BCG, 2024)
- Employees who feel included are 50% less likely to leave
- Companies in top quartile for inclusion outperform peers by 36% in profitability
- Inclusive employers access 40% larger talent pools
- Bias-driven decisions create litigation exposure and reputational harm

## Key Metrics
- Hiring Funnel Parity (target: ±2% variance at each stage) - Equal conversion rates
- Promotion Rate Parity (target: ±2% of representation) - After controlling for performance/tenure
- Pay Equity Adjusted Gap (target: <2% unexplained variance) - After controlling for role/level/performance
- Retention Parity (target: ±3% variance) - Equal voluntary attrition across demographics
- Representation by Level - Proportional to available talent pool
- Belonging Score (target: >80% across all demographics) - "I feel I belong here"

## Notebooks

### 1. Hiring Funnel Equity Analysis
Identifies where bias enters the hiring process.

**Key outputs:** Drop-off rates at each stage by demographic, adverse impact analysis, time-to-hire disparities

### 2. Promotion Equity & Pay Gap Analysis
Surfaces "glass ceiling" effects and pay disparities.

**Key outputs:** Promotion rates by demographic, time-to-promotion analysis, regression-controlled pay equity, representation at each level

### 3. Retention Equity & Belonging Analysis
Tracks retention gaps that signal exclusion.

**Key outputs:** Voluntary attrition by demographic, exit interview themes, belonging scores, retention of high performers

## Data Requirements
- HRIS: Demographics, job level, department, tenure, promotion history
- ATS: Hiring funnel data (applications, screens, interviews, offers)
- Compensation: Salary, bonus, equity, compa-ratios
- Performance Management: Ratings, promotion readiness
- Attrition Data: Exits, exit interviews
- Engagement Surveys: Inclusion/belonging questions with demographic cuts

## Best Practices
- Run annual pay equity audits with regression controls
- Use structured hiring processes (standardized interviews, diverse panels, blind screening)
- Establish clear promotion criteria and calibration sessions
- Hold managers accountable for team inclusion outcomes
- Conduct intersectional analysis (race × gender, disability × LGBTQ+)
- Combine quantitative data with qualitative insights (focus groups, listening sessions)
- Budget for corrections when gaps are found
