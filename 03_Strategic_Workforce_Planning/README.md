# Strategic Workforce Planning

## Overview
Ensures the organization has the right people with the right skills in the right roles at the right time. Forecasts future workforce needs, identifies skills gaps, and develops action plans to close them.

## Business Impact
- Without SWP: Scrambling to fill critical roles, skills shortages delay launches, reactive hiring
- With SWP: Proactive talent pipelines, early skills gap identification, optimized headcount allocation
- Example: AI product launch planned 18 months ahead saves $5M in delayed revenue and $200K in recruiting costs through upskilling and pipeline development

## Key Metrics
- Headcount Forecast Accuracy (target: ±5% variance) - Validates planning assumptions
- Critical Role Fill Rate (target: >70%) - % of critical roles with identified successors
- Skills Gap Index (target: <20%) - % of required skills not available internally
- Internal Mobility Rate (target: >30%) - % of roles filled internally vs externally
- Succession Bench Strength (target: >1.5) - Average ready successors per critical role
- Span of Control (target: 5-9) - Direct reports per manager

## Notebooks

### 1. Headcount & Capacity Forecasting
Predicts future staffing needs based on business plans.

**Key outputs:** 3-year workforce plan by department, quarterly hiring roadmap, capacity modeling, scenario analysis

### 2. Skills Gap Analysis
Identifies current vs future skills needed to execute strategy.

**Key outputs:** Skills heat map by department, emerging skills requirements, build vs buy recommendations, training ROI

### 3. Succession Planning
Ensures critical roles have identified, ready successors.

**Key outputs:** Critical role inventory, successor heat map with readiness levels, development action plans

### 4. Span of Control & Organizational Design
Optimizes organizational structure for efficiency and scalability.

**Key outputs:** Management ratio analysis, span of control distribution, organizational layers, efficiency recommendations

### 5. Retirement & Knowledge Transfer Risk
Identifies and mitigates institutional knowledge loss.

**Key outputs:** Retirement risk by department, critical knowledge holders, knowledge transfer plans

### 6. Internal Mobility & Talent Flow
Optimizes internal movement to fill roles faster and retain talent.

**Key outputs:** Internal mobility rate by department, career progression paths, skills adjacency map

## Data Requirements
- HRIS: Employee count, department, job title, location, manager
- Business Plans: Revenue targets, product roadmap, headcount budget
- Skills Inventory: Employee skills, certifications, project experience
- Talent Reviews: High potential designation, readiness level, career aspirations
- Recruiting Data: Time-to-fill, internal vs external hires, hiring velocity

## Best Practices
- Align to business strategy (start with "What is the business trying to achieve?")
- Use multiple forecasting methods (driver-based models, trend analysis, manager input)
- Plan in scenarios (best case, base case, worst case)
- Refresh regularly (quarterly reviews, annual updates)
- Focus on critical roles first (10-20% that are business-critical)
- Build before you need (identify skills gaps 12-24 months ahead)
