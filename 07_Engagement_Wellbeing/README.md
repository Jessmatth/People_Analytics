# Employee Engagement & Wellbeing

## Overview
Measures employee engagement levels, identifies burnout risks, and tracks wellbeing trends to maintain a healthy, productive workforce. Uses Gallup Q12 framework and WHO burnout guidelines.

## Business Impact
- Highly engaged teams show 21% greater profitability (Gallup, 2020)
- Employees experiencing burnout are 2.6x more likely to leave
- Early intervention prevents costly turnover and performance decline
- Engagement is a leading indicator of retention and productivity

## Notebooks

### 1. Engagement Survey Analysis
Analyzes pulse survey results to identify engagement drivers and at-risk segments.

**Key outputs:** eNPS scores, engagement distribution by department/tenure, driver analysis, segmentation

### 2. Wellbeing & Burnout Risk
Identifies employees at risk of burnout using work hours, stress indicators, and survey responses.

**Key outputs:** Burnout risk scores, work-life balance assessment, correlation with performance/retention

### 3. Engagement Trends & Actions
Tracks engagement over time and evaluates intervention effectiveness with critical assessment.

**Key outputs:** Quarterly trends, intervention impact analysis (with honest failure assessment), ROI calculations

## Data Requirements
- Quarterly pulse surveys (engagement, wellbeing, eNPS)
- HRIS data (work hours, PTO usage, flexibility)
- Performance ratings
- Attrition records
- Manager effectiveness scores
