# Compensation Equity & Benchmarking

## Overview
Analyzes pay equity, performs market benchmarking, and optimizes total rewards strategy to ensure competitive and fair compensation.

## Business Impact
- Pay equity prevents legal risk and reputational damage
- Employees paid below market are 3.5x more likely to leave
- Strategic total rewards mix maximizes value per dollar spent
- Proactive equity audits prevent costly lawsuits and corrections

## Key Metrics
- Compa-Ratio (target: 0.95-1.05) - Actual salary divided by midpoint of pay range
- Market Positioning - Percentile of company pay vs external market
- Pay Equity Gap (target: <2% unexplained variance) - Regression-controlled analysis
- Pay-for-Performance Correlation (target: r > 0.5) - Link between performance and compensation
- Total Rewards Value - Combined value of base, bonus, equity, and benefits

## Notebooks

### 1. Pay Equity Analysis
Identifies and quantifies unexplained pay gaps across demographic groups.

**Key outputs:** Regression-controlled pay equity, pay-for-performance correlation, compa-ratio distribution, outlier identification

### 2. Market Benchmarking Analysis
Compares internal pay to external market data to ensure competitiveness.

**Key outputs:** Market positioning by role/level/location, compression analysis, geographic differentials, retention risk

### 3. Total Rewards Optimization
Analyzes complete compensation package and optimizes mix of cash vs benefits.

**Key outputs:** Total rewards calculation, cost-per-employee by segment, benefits utilization and value, budget reallocation scenarios

## Data Requirements
- HRIS: Compensation data (base salary, bonus, equity grants)
- External market surveys: Radford, Mercer, Payscale
- Benefits cost and utilization data
- Performance review ratings
- Demographic data for equity analysis

## Best Practices
- Run annual pay equity audits with regression controls
- Proactively budget for corrections before gaps become large
- Use market data to set competitive pay ranges
- Ensure pay correlates with performance ratings
- Communicate audit results and action plans for transparency
- Maintain strict salary data confidentiality
